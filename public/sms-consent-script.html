SMS Consent Script

We Move On Demand, LLC  |  For staff use during booking calls

About This Script

The following script is read by our team to obtain verbal SMS consent from customers during the booking call. Consent must be obtained before any post-move text messages are sent.

Verbal Consent Script

Read the following to the customer verbatim before ending the booking call:

"After your move is completed, we will send you a text message asking for a Google review. Message frequency is approximately 1–2 messages per completed move. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance. Do you agree to receive this text message?"

Important: Customer consent must be recorded in the company CRM immediately after the call. Log the customer's name, phone number, date and time of consent, and the staff member who obtained it. Do not send any SMS messages unless consent is confirmed and logged.
